About Us

Welcome to the heart of our college's social life—The Durga Canteen. We pride ourselves on offering a diverse menu of delicious and affordable meals, with options to cater to every taste and dietary need. Our canteen is not just a place to eat; it's a gathering spot for students to enjoy quality food in a cozy and vibrant atmosphere.

Join us for a culinary adventure, special events, and a sense of community that makes dining here a memorable experience. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your college journey.
